Kundamangalam Co-Operative Rural Bank

The Kunnamangalam Co-operative Rural Bank was started in 31st July 1946 with the Kunnamangalam, kuruvattoor Panjayath and mayanad cherukulathur amshangal limits as its area of operation. From a humble beginning the Bank has made great progress with in a span of 76 years and is at par with some of the major banks in the Rural area.
